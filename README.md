# [POWER BI] CRM Sales Opportunities Dashboard

---

# I. Introduction

## 1. Introduction to Dataset

Sales opportunity management is one of the most important activities in CRM systems because it directly impacts revenue growth and sales performance.

This project analyzes CRM sales opportunities to monitor:
- sales pipeline health
- opportunity conversion performance
- product effectiveness
- sales team productivity
- industry and regional performance

The dataset includes:
- sales opportunity records
- customer account information
- product details
- sales team structure

The dashboard was designed to support senior sales managers in making strategic sales and business decisions using data-driven insights.

## 3. Business Questions

The dashboard was designed to answer the following business questions:

- Is the current sales pipeline strong enough to achieve revenue targets?
- Which products generate the highest revenue and win rate?
- Which industries contribute the most successful opportunities?
- Which sales teams and managers perform best?
- Which stages of the sales process have the highest opportunity loss?
- How does sales performance change over time?
